TheBigCollegePlan

"So you're planning on college…"

First, you're in a good place because everyone is planning for the future; prospective students want to progress their dreams and parents have made provisions to start their journey.

Second, even if this family time may be a bit stressful with differing perspectives on what's best, you know it's "gonna work out" because everyone is generally aiming in the same direction.

At this critical time, it is important for families to remember everyone wants to do the right thing even though it is often difficult to understand the array of issues and explain opinions. No matter what school or institution is your ultimate goal, there will be a "good fit" for both students and parents.

The College Selector will help both students and parents to raise issues, see and compare them across diverse school offerings. College Selector will help you form a "picture" assisting family discussions to make this exciting choice for the future.
